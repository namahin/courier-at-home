Frequently asked Questions

General Queries:

  • 1. Who we are?

    Courier @ Home as a whole is a web-based E-commerce shipping solutions provider, or to put it into simple words, we provide easy-access solution for delivery service. With an extensive nationwide delivery network and super skilled manpower, we are proud to position ourselves as Bangladesh’s premier express delivery service. More than 50,000 people and businesses in Bangladesh are using Courier @ Home. Our platform allows you to book for delivery from any type of business in Bangladesh and of course, at an affordable rate.

  • 2. How do I reach you?

    We are available 24/7 at 09643100100 or you can email us at care@courierathome.com. Besides, you can also connect with us on Facebook.

  • 3. What kind of product Courier @ Home deliver?

    Courier @ Home deliver all legal portable products including mobile, documents, parcel etc. except perishable items.

  • 4. If I want to change address or any other information about the parcel after giving you what should I do?

    You can call our hotline number (09643100100) to make the changes.

  • 5. Is there any off day for parcel delivery?

    Courier @ Home does not have any weekend for any of its services. We are available 24/7 to assist you. However, the service may remain off in some public holidays.

  • 6. What is your timeline for product delivery?

    Inside Dhaka, we deliver the product within 24 hours.
    Inside Dhaka Suburb, we deliver the product within 48 hours.
    Outside Dhaka, we deliver the product within 72 Hours.
    N.B. Delivery time may vary depending on external conditions or customer availability.

  • 7. What is Courier @ Home’s pickup time?

    Our pickup timeframe inside Dhaka is 2pm to 8pm.

  • 8. Can you do product exchange from customers?

    Sorry, currently we are not giving this service.

Servicer Oriented:

  • 9. How can I get Data Entry Service?

    If you want to avail this service, please contact our hotline number 09643100100 or email us at care@courierathome.com. Our associated team will contact with you.

  • 10. Will I have to pay for this service?

    No, it is completely free for the conveyance our merchant.
    N.B. Please note that you will be only avail this service if you have more than 500 parcels in a month.

  • 11. What are Packaging Services?

    The packaging of goods is an essential part of the distribution process. It is difficult to package the items in a way that is both appealing and safe for delivery. With this in mind, Courier @ Home has produced the ultimate packaging solution. By ordering packaging materials from us, such as boxes of different sizes, poly bags, and bubble wrap, you can easily pack your items in a clear and secure manner.

  • 12. How can I order for the packaging materials?

    If you want to avail this service, please contact our hotline number 09643100100 or email us at care@courierathome.com.

  • 13. What is fragile and liquid item delivery service?

    Fragile & Liquid Item Delivery is a service that delivers items that are fragile and liquid in nature. Because of the sensitivity of the quality of the product, we will ensure the protection of the products during delivery. Merchants are guaranteed full credit for parcels booked via this service.

  • 14. Which types of product do you consider as a fragile product?

    The term "fragile products" refers to packages that are prone to breaking. Fragile refers to a material or construction that is delicate and needs special handling. The term "liquid objects" refers to goods that are liquid in nature. Fragile and Liquid items include but not limited to the followin:
    ⦁ Ornaments
    ⦁ Cosmetics Products
    ⦁ Handicraft Items made of wood and bamboo.
    ⦁ Ceramic and Glass made products.
    Any kind of liquid containing product.

  • 15. Is fragile item delivery service only applicable for inside Dhaka?

    Yes, currently this service is available for inside Dhaka only.

  • 16. What will be the charge for fragile item delivery booking?

    No, it is completely free for the conveyance our merchant.

Damage/lost issue:

  • 17. What is the compensation for the damage/lost products?

    In case of lost and damaged parcels or courier, you must inform us immediately. Once we receive your complain, we will thoroughly investigate the matter as soon as possible. This process will take minimum of 5 working days. If any parcel is lost or damaged, Courier @ Home will be responsible for full compensation of the parcel.

  • 18. When should I inform you if an item is lost or damaged?

    Any missing or damaged objects must be reported to us as soon as they are discovered. It may happen during delivery or upon return. We limit the amount of time you have to raise a problem to 5 days.

  • 19. What type of products are not covered by the compensation/ refund system?

    If the product is damaged as a result of inadequate packaging, there will be no refund. The refund scheme does not extend to liquid products such as ghee, honey, gasoline, or any glass bottles that have been destroyed.
    In addition, if the product being shipped is defective from the start, the consumer must inspect it in front of the rider and return it. Complaints of any type are not necessary after a satisfactory home delivery or return.

Payment:

  • 20. What is the payment cycle for merchants?

    We have a five-day payment period (Sunday to Thursday). The payment will be sent to you the next working day after the package is delivered.

  • 21. How will I receive my merchant payment?

    Within the next working day, you will be paid for all package deliveries made on a specific day, either through your bank account or bKash account.
